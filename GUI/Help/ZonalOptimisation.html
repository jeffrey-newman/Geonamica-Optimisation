Zonal optimiation parameters

With the zonal optimisation functionality, the optimiser attempts to build a zonal policy. To do this, the entire modelling domain is broken up into small spatial areas. The optimiser selects a set of these small areas and combines them to form a region, and specifies a zonal policy for that region, that results in the best objective values


Map specifying zones:

This needs to specify a gdal-compatible raster that divides the geoproject region into small spatial areas. The larger the number of small areas, the finer the resolution of the zonal policy region that will be created by the optimisation.
